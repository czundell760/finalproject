MNTLTY

MNTLTY, or Mentality, is a movement dedicated to being real, being positive, and being creative.

MNTLTY was established in 2020 by me, Carl Zundell, as a movement to spread positivity through visuals and messages into the minds of the youth. The most important concepts that I believe in are simplicity and minimalism because I believe that they make people appreciate what they have, despite how little and how simple it is. These concepts can be found in my art and my work. I hope you enjoy.
